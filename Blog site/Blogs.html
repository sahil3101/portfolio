Jio Phone

Verdict

Reliance has revolutionised the telecommunication industry with its affordable internet plans and 4G VoLTE compatible keypad mobiles and smartphones. JioPhone Next is yet another interesting launch by the brand, made available under 5k. You will not only get to experience seamless entertainment with the device but can also capture decent images and make video calls to near and dear ones. The best part is, Reliance JioPhone Next offers dual-SIM connectivity with a dedicated microSD card slot.


Jio Phone Next is an upcoming mobile by Jio. The phone is rumoured to come with a 5.50-inch touchscreen display. It come with 2GB of RAM. The Jio Phone Next is rumoured to run Android and is expected to be powered by a 2500mAh battery.


As far as the cameras are concerned, the Jio Phone Next is rumoured to pack a single rear camera setup. It is rumoured to sport a single front camera setup for selfies.


Connectivity options on the Jio Phone Next are said to include Wi-Fi, GPS, 3G, and 4G (with support for Band 40 used by some LTE networks in India). Sensors on the phone are rumoured to include accelerometer.
